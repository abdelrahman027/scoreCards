Beblin Assessment Roles

Coordinator (CO)
The Coordinator is a mature individual who is good at delegating tasks and promoting the team’s interests. They are good at organizing and managing the team’s activities and facilitating meetings.
Shaper (SH)
The Shaper is dynamic, outgoing, and highly motivated. They challenge the team to improve and overcome obstacles. They are a driving force in the team and are keen to push the team forward.
Plant (PL)
The Plant is creative and innovative. They come up with original ideas and solutions to problems. They are often the source of new and imaginative approaches.
Monitor Evaluator (ME)
The Monitor Evaluator is analytical and objective. They provide a logical overview and evaluate the team’s ideas and proposals. They are good at spotting potential problems.
Resource Investigator (RI)
The Resource Investigator is outgoing and enthusiastic. They explore new opportunities, develop contacts, and gather information from outside the team. They are good at networking.
Implementer (IM)
The Implementer is practical and efficient. They turn ideas into action and make sure tasks are completed. They are good at organizing work and getting things done.
Completer Finisher (CF)
he Completer Finisher is detail-oriented and ensures that the team’s work is completed accurately and on time. They focus on quality control and making sure tasks are completed to a high standard.
Teamworker (TW)
The Teamworker is supportive and cooperative. They help build relationships within the team and resolve conflicts. They are good at facilitating teamwork and ensuring everyone works well together.
Specialist (SP)
The Specialist has specialized knowledge and skills in a specific area. They provide in-depth expertise and contribute technical insights and solutions.
